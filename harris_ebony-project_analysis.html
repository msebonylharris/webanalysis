Sites Intent or Purpose!

Back to Top

In today's society web, surfers are accustomed to view state of the art websites that are not only nice to look at but also user friendly. In this Analysis, we will address the need and importance of a website that caters to the "potential user." The Websites main purpose is to provide information and Links to the Parents, Students and Teachers of Spring Lake Elementary School. In addition, our goal for this web re-design will be to make the website user-friendly and provide an overall better user experience. We also plan on increasing the sites traffic.

We intend on achieving our goals by making the website more compact while at the same time making it easier for the user to navigate to their destination. Although the website is currently cluttered, we know that this website is being used. We do not want someone to have trouble finding information on the new site. To ease the transition to the new website, we have decided to place a "Find it Quick" tab in the Navigation. This way, the user can find what they are looking for without having to go through the entire website.

Competition Analysis

Back to Top

I looked at the Villages Charter School, and Berkely Charter School as competitors. I noticed that they had pages that were easy to navigate as and for the most part, user friendly. I felt that the colors used by Berkley Charter school were a little too loud for a school website, but it has a great layout. I like the way Berkley adds its calendar and news to the front page. This is something that I hope to incorporate into my website. I noticed how The Villages Charter School used side navigation. Although this looks good, I do not feel that it would work for what we are trying to achieve. I believe that the layout Berkley Charter school makes a perfect example of the elementary school website I intend to implement.
